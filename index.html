About Me

Nice to meet you! Maybe you do not know me yet so I will introduce myself a bit. I like to follow my intuition and go into things that I feel are meant to be. This is for example the reason why I played amateur theater for eight years or raised an assistance dog. In terms of design, I believe that creating anything works as therapy. You just forget the time and don't think about anything else. To balance the mental work, I like to do sports. My favourites are tennis, cycling and yoga.

During my career, I worked for a couple of software companies and e-commerce companies. There I realized that the path to a satisfied customer leads through simplicity, functionality and authenticity. I am convinced, that if UX design follows these rules, the website / app is headed for success.

In the coming period, I would like to focus more on animation and motion design in my digital work. I like how this type of technology can help convey more than just words.

If you are interested in cooperation regarding UX design, I can process all parts of the design creation for you - from brief, moodboard, empathy map, customer journey map, storymapping, wireframe, mockup, prototype, testing to design optimization.
